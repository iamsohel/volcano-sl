Who We Are

Therap Services is a US based company with an office in Dhaka, Bangladesh. We have been operating since 2003. Therap is a global team of professionals with diverse expertise working together across USA and Bangladesh. We believe that Therap as a company, service and product strives to make a difference in the lives of people and helps to make it a better world.

group_work

WHAT WE DO

Therap is the National leader in providing Software as a Service (SaaS) to government and private organizations supporting people with developmental disabilities in the US. We have built an Internet-scale application using Java EE technologies which accommodates nearly half a million people. In collaboration with our US colleagues, we actively shape the future of the product.

WORK LIFE AT THERAP

Therap strives to maintain a startup culture in which everyone is a hands-on contributor and feels comfortable sharing ideas and opinions. With its own space situated at the heart of Banani, it has over 130 employees. From an excellent working environment to transport facilities to recreation and healthy meals, Therap ensures a workplace that people love to join.
